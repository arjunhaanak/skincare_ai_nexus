About the Project

Skincare AI Nexus is a next-generation healthcare solution that leverages deep learning to provide instant skin disease assessments.

Our platform uses the highly efficient VGG16 Convolutional Neural Network, pre-trained on millions of images and fine-tuned specifically for dermatological conditions. By automating the screening process, we aim to provide early detection support for critical conditions like Melanoma while offering guidance for minor conditions.

System Specifications
  • Architecture: CNN (VGG16)
  • Target Classes: 7 Skin Diseases
  • Image Size: 224 x 224 (RGB)
  • Optimizer: Adam
  • Loss Function: Categorical Crossentropy
